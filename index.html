With 7 years of experience working with both athletic and general populations as a physiotherapist and strength & conditioning coach, I bring a combined performance and rehabilitation perspective to every client I work with.

My Philosophy: Strength Above All Else

Too often, injuries are approached with a short-term mindset: reduce pain, reduce swelling, get moving. While these steps are important, they’re only the beginning.

Real rehabilitation—the kind that prevents recurrence and elevates you beyond where you started—comes from rebuilding strength in a targeted, progressive, and methodical way. My aim is not only to get you out of pain but to help you become stronger, more resilient, and better prepared for the demands of your sport or daily life.
